Tic Tac Toe RULES

Two Player Game

  • Two players take turns marking the spaces in a 3x3 grid with X or O.
  • Players take turns placing their marks in empty squares, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  • The first player to get three of their marks in a row wins.
  • The game is also over when all nine squares are full and neither player has three in a row, resulting in a draw.

Against the computer

  • You and the computer take turns marking the spaces in a 3x3 grid with X or O; You being X.
  • You and the computer take turns placing your marks in empty squares, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  • The first player to get three of their marks in a row wins.
  • The game is also over when all nine squares are full and neither your nor the computer has three in a row, resulting in a draw.
