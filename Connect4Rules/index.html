Connect 4 Rules

Players must take turns dropping one of their circles from the top into a grid.

The pieces fall straight down and occupy the lowest available space within the column.

The objective of the game is to be the first to form a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of four of one's own color circles.

The game ends when there is a 4 in a row or a tie.
